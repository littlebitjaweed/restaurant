About Us




Born in Berlin, Germany in the year 1955, William "Wolfgang" Blazkovich Stein was a professional chef who specialized in a wide variety of cuisines ranging from French, German, Italian to Indian.

After opening his first restaurant in Berlin, he then went on to specialize in Indian cuisine and since then expanded into several countries across the world. His legacy is carried on by his chain of restaurants today.
