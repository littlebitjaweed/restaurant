About Us



Born in Berlin, Germany in the year 1955, William "Wolfgang" Blazkovich Stein was a professional chef who specialized in a wide variety of cuisines ranging from French, German, Italian to Indian.

After opening his first restaurant in Berlin, he then went on to specialize in Indian cuisine and since then expanded into several countries across the world. His legacy is carried on by his chain of restaurants today.

William “Wolfgang” Blazkovich Stein’s culinary journey began in the bustling streets of Berlin, where he was born in 1955. From a young age, Wolfgang was fascinated by the diverse flavors and aromas that filled his home, thanks to his mother’s exceptional cooking skills. This early exposure to the culinary arts ignited a passion in him that would shape his future. After completing his formal education, Wolfgang decided to pursue his dream of becoming a professional chef. He enrolled in a prestigious culinary school in France, where he honed his skills in French cuisine, mastering the delicate balance of flavors and techniques that define this culinary tradition. Upon returning to Berlin, Wolfgang opened his first restaurant, a quaint bistro that quickly gained a reputation for its exquisite French and German dishes. However, Wolfgang’s insatiable curiosity and desire to explore new culinary horizons led him to travel extensively across Europe and Asia. It was during his travels in India that he discovered a profound love for Indian cuisine. The vibrant spices, rich textures, and complex flavors captivated him, and he decided to specialize in this new culinary frontier. Wolfgang’s dedication to mastering Indian cuisine paid off when he opened his first Indian restaurant in Berlin. The restaurant was an instant success, drawing food enthusiasts from all over the city.

Encouraged by this success, Wolfgang expanded his culinary empire, opening restaurants in major cities across Europe, Asia, and North America.

Each restaurant was a testament to his commitment to authenticity and excellence, offering diners an unforgettable culinary experience. Today, Wolfgang’s legacy lives on through his chain of restaurants, which continue to delight patrons with their diverse and innovative menus. His influence on the culinary world is undeniable, and his story serves as an inspiration to aspiring chefs everywhere. Wolfgang’s journey from a young boy in Berlin to a world-renowned chef is a testament to the power of passion, dedication, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.
